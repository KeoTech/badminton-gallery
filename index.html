About

Ever since I became a varsity badminton player during my Junior High School years, I have admired many world-class athletes in the sport. Their discipline, speed, power, and passion inspired me to train harder and continue improving. This gallery is a simple tribute to the players I watched while growing up — the legends who pushed me to love and respect the sport even more.
